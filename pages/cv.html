Interactive Resume

I’m currently a postdoctoral researcher working for the European project “Extending sensorimotor contingencies to cognition” (EU 7th Framework; ICT: Cognitive Systems and Robotics). In my academic work I use a combination of AI methods, including neural networks and genetic algorithms, as well as physical simulations to test hypotheses regarding human and robot motor control. I have also worked as commercial software engineer and team lead on adaptive character simulation and AI for NaturalMotion. My academic background and interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, cognitive science and software development in general (C++, Python, Matlab, R …).

I’m now looking to apply my skills and experience with AI, machine learning and commercial software development in the field of data science/big data.
